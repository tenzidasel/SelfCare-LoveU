Scented Candles

Benefits:

1. It can reduce anxiety and stress, improve sleep quality, improve symptoms of depression, and even potentially reduce inflammation.
2. Floral and herbal aromas, like geranium, lavender, and peppermint, have been proven to be useful for the treatment of various psychological and physiological disorders through the burning of aromatherapeutic candles.
3. Specific scents in candles can stimulate memory in the brain.

Essential Oils

Benefits:

1. Essential oils can be added to your bathwater.Bathing is a beautiful way to cleanse, relax and unwind. 2. The practice of massage can be dramatically enhanced when essential oils are implemented into the massage session correctly and sensibly. 3. Use of essential oils during meditation and yoga can enhance the experience by grounding you and promoting a sense of calm.
